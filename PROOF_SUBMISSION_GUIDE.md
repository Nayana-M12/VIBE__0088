## â“ FAQ

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Most proofs are reviewed within 24 hours. Energy and water bills are auto-approved instantly.

**Q: Can I submit the same proof twice?**
A: No, duplicate submissions are automatically rejected.

**Q: What if my proof is rejected?**
A: You'll see the rejection reason. Fix the issue and resubmit with a new, valid proof.

**Q: Can I delete a submission?**
A: Pending submissions can be cancelled. Approved submissions cannot be deleted.

**Q: Do I need to post on social feed?**
A: No! Proof submission is completely separate from social posts. Submit privately.

**Q: What happens to my ecoBits if rejected?**
A: No ecoBits are deducted. You simply don't earn the reward until approved proof is submitted.

**Q: Can I submit old bills?**
A: Proof should be recent (within 30 days) to ensure current eco-actions.

**Q: Is my personal information safe?**
A: Yes! You can hide account numbers and sensitive data. We only need date, amount, and eco-action proof.
